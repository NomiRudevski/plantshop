Q&A

How often should I water my plants?

It depends on the type of plant and its environment. Generally, it's good to water when the top inch of soil is dry.

What's the best lighting for tropical plants?

Tropical plants thrive in bright, indirect sunlight. Avoid placing them in direct sunlight as it can scorch their leaves.

How can I prevent pests on my plants?

Regularly inspect your plants for signs of pests such as aphids or spider mites. You can use natural remedies like neem oil or insecticidal soap to control infestations.

Why are the leaves of my plant turning yellow?

Yellowing leaves can be caused by overwatering, underwatering, insufficient light, or nutrient deficiencies. Assess the plant's care routine to identify the cause.

How do I repot my plant?

Choose a pot that is slightly larger than the current one and has drainage holes. Gently remove the plant from its current pot, loosen the roots, place it in the new pot, and fill with fresh potting mix.
